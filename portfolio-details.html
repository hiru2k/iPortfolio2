Project Details

This multifaceted application offers a comprehensive suite of tools to enrich and understand users' daily routines like sleep quality analysis, intelligent user location prediction, emotion recognition and monitoring and eating behavior analysis. Location prediction is one of the most important and user location prediction feature dynamically anticipates the user's next location, displaying it prominently on-screen. Adjacent to the predicted location, a map showcases nearby points of interest—such as restaurants, hospitals, and supermarkets—offering users convenience and accessibility options. These locations are categorized and displayed alongside exclusive offers specific to each place, enhancing the user experience. Additionally, users have access to their history of previously visited locations for quick reference and revisit. Utilizing graph representation, a comprehensive report details the total travel time over the week, analyzing daily travel durations. This feature provides users with insightful analytics, aiding in better planning and optimizing travel schedules based on their weekly routines.

Project Details

Companies interested in showcasing their offers and special events can register on the website. To make the registration process user-friendly, the website provides a smart form that automatically captures the precise coordinates of the company's physical location. In the application, users have the capability to handle their offers, allowing them to create, modify, and remove offers. When they wish to add a new offer, they also have the option to include an image. Within the user profile section, users have the ability to manage and update their profiles. users can easily edit their company's location by simply moving the map marker to the desired position, ensuring that the coordinates are promptly adjusted to the newly selected location.
